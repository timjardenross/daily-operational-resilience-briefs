# Daily Operational Resilience Intelligence Brief

**Date:** 2026-06-19

## Key Incidents

### Vodafone Australia Network Outage
- **Date:** June 18, 2026
- Major nationwide outage affecting millions of customers due to power failure at a network hub around 8am AEST.
- Services progressively restored by mid-morning.
- Highlights risks in telco infrastructure resilience and single points of failure.

## Regulatory Context (CPS 230)
- APRA's Prudential Standard CPS 230 on Operational Risk Management emphasizes identification, escalation, and management of operational risk incidents.
- Entities must notify APRA within 72 hours of material incidents.
- Full compliance deadlines approaching for non-SFIs by July 2026.

## Implications
- Third-party and infrastructure dependencies remain critical vulnerabilities.
- Need for robust business continuity and incident response plans.

## Recommendations
- Review critical service provider contracts per CPS 230.
- Conduct regular resilience testing and scenario planning.
- Enhance monitoring of network hubs and power redundancies.
