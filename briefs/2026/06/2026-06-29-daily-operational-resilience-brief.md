## Executive Summary
Regulatory implementation remains the dominant theme, with APRA’s targeted amendments to CPS 230 Operational Risk Management taking effect 1 July 2026, emphasizing material service provider oversight, exemptions for non-traditional providers, and enhanced contractual resilience requirements. Cyber threats to operational technology and supply chains persist, exemplified by the recent ransomware disruption to Mackay Sugar’s milling and rail operations in Queensland. Telco and power vulnerabilities continue to surface, while geopolitical risks—particularly energy supply shocks and funding volatility—feature prominently in RBA and APRA assessments. No major natural hazards are currently impacting Australia, but compounding climate and infrastructure risks warrant ongoing vigilance. Overall, the environment underscores the need for robust third-party management, scenario testing, and cross-sector coordination to maintain critical operations.
